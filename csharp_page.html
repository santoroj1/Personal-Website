Star Odyssey

Shortly after learning the basics of C# and the XNA Game Framework (4.0) in 2013, my friend Talon and I designed Star Odyssey, a fast-paced arcade game that pits two players against each other in an all-out war. The game features a menu with a leaderboard table, a tutorial, and a large battlefield complete with functioning AIs for each side. We oversaw all elements of the design phase, including planning, graphic design, coding, and debugging. I was responsible for most of the game-related programming, while my friend Talon completed all of the graphic design and the programming for the menu.
